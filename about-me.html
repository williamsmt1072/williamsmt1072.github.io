About me

My name is Mitchell Williams. I was born in 1997 in Murray, Utah. Shortly after I was born I moved to southern California where my father completed his medical residency. After this, I moved back to Salt Lake City, Utah then moved to Riverton, Utah over a year later. I have lived in Riverton for 20 years. In my sophomore year of High School, I attended Riverton High School then moved to Itineris Early College High School where I graduated. During my time at Itineris, I enrolled in primary college-level classes working towards my associate's degree. In the summer after my high school graduation, I completed my associate of science at Salt Lake Community College. Following the completion of my associate degree, I transferred to the University of Utah. Currently, I am working towards my bachelor's degree in Biomedical Engineering with a biomechanical emphasis. During this time I started my own business doing freelance graphic and web design. Vortex Design started in 2020 at the beginning of the COVID-19 pandemic primarily making posters and banners. The business began to expand taking on contracting work from small businesses and medical organizations. I additionally work full-time for Foot and Ankle Specialists, PLLC as a medical assistant.
